CoderDojo Indigo Mission


The monthly activity will follow the CoderDojo methodology and philosophy to create a collaborative and fun volunteer-led computer club. Scratch, a visual-based online programming environment developed by MIT, will be utilized to empower students to quickly experiment and create interactive projects - animations, games, music and art.

Along with guided learning at each session, students will have time to freely experiment with programming and creative visual tools. Take-home material may also be supplied to encourage everyone to continue projects on their own time.

At the end of the experience, the aim is for students to have had a fun time learning how computer applications and games are developed, by making their own using art, math and logic.

 

We hope that students gain a new perspective on technology as creators (not just consumers) and a desire to continue learning.
