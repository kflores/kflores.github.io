Projects

Mission

CoderDojo Indigo is an introductory level coding experience for 4th-8th graders at Indigo Program held monthly during the 2014/2015 school year starting in October.

This collaborative and volunteer-led computer club follows the CoderDojo philosophy. We are using Scratch, an MIT developed online visual programming environment, to empower students to quickly experiment and create interactive projects - animations, games, music and art.

Through the experience, the aim is for students to have a fun time learning how computer applications and games are developed by making their own.

Requirements

Please note the requirements before signing up for a session.

  • Sign up for a free account at the Scratch website (http://scratch.mit.edu/) and bring your login!
  • Since we are sponsored by the Indigo Program, session sign-ups will be through school process.
  • Students must bring a fully-charged WiFi-capable laptop.
  • Students can bring water, but should be fed before-hand.
  • Students must be accompanied by an adult during full session
    (No siblings, please).
