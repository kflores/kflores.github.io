Introduction

Welcome to CoderDojo Indigo - an introductory level coding experience for 4th-8th graders at Indigo School. We will hold monthly sessions during the school year starting in October of 2014. It is not mandatory to attend every session, but it will be useful for students to build upon their knowledge and experience.

 

Schedule

Each session will be held on an evening from 6:30-8:30pm at Indigo.

  • April 13, 2015 (Monday)
  • May 11, 2015 (Monday)

Requirements

Please note the requirements before signing up for a session.

  • Sign up for a free account at the Scratch website (http://scratch.mit.edu/) and bring your login! This will allow you to save your projects so that you can continue working from home and in later sessions.
  • Students must bring a fully-charged WiFi-capable laptop.
  • Students can bring water, but should be fed before-hand.
  • Students must be accompanied by an adult during full session
    (No siblings, please).
